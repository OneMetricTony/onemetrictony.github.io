Kavosh AI & Robotics Academy · Canada

KAVOSH — Engineering World Champions Since 2010

Kavosh AI & Robotics Academy was founded in 2010 in Canada, with hubs across Toronto, Markham, Vancouver, Richmond, Winnipeg and Montreal. We train the next generation of engineers for RoboCup, FIRA and VEX — building humanoid and rescue robots, and competing on the world stage.

Who We Are

From a small group of teenage builders to a Canadian national robotics program, Kavosh has spent more than a decade turning curiosity into engineering excellence. Our founders began competing internationally at age 14 — and our students now carry that legacy onto the global stage at the FIRA RoboWorld Cup 2026 in Markham.

Our Journey

Timeline

2026 · FIRA RoboWorld Cup (Markham, Canada)

The world comes to Canada. Kavosh competes at the first FIRA RoboWorld Cup ever hosted in Canada.

2025 · FIRA Canada National (Markham)

National competition held in Markham, Ontario (March 15–16) — preparing the next wave of champions on home soil.

2024 · FIRA World Cup (São Luís, Brazil)

Competing on the world stage in Brazil, deepening our humanoid and rescue robotics programs against the best teams on the planet.

2023 · FIRA Germany (Wolfenbüttel)

Kavosh fields the first Canadian national team at the FIRA RoboWorld Cup — bringing home 2 championships and 1 runner-up finish.

2010

Kavosh AI & Robotics Academy is founded in Canada. Founders begin competing internationally at age 14 — earning 10 championships and 24 trophies over the years.
