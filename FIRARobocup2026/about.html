About the Organizer

Meet the person leading sponsorship for the team's FIRA RoboWorld Cup 2026 effort in Markham, Ontario.

Organizer

Sponsorship Lead

Tony Chu

Tony Chu is the sponsorship lead for the FIRA RoboWorld Cup 2026 effort in Markham, Canada. He works with partners and supporters to help bring world-class robotics competition to the community and to connect sponsors with the next generation of robotics and AI talent.

For sponsorship inquiries, partnership opportunities, or general questions, Tony is the primary point of contact and is happy to help.
