💡 PROBLEM TIPS

MOMENTUMp = mv. Heavy + fast = hard to stop. Units kg·m/s.
COLLISIONSAlways conserve momentum: total p before = total p after. Elastic also keeps KE; inelastic loses some (objects may stick).
IMPULSEF·Δt = Δp. Same Δp: longer Δt → smaller force. That's the airbag.
FRICTIONf = μN. Static μ_s (not moving yet) ≥ kinetic μ_k (sliding). On flat ground N = mg.
FREE-BODYDraw a dot for the object, then every force as an arrow: gravity (down), normal (up), applied, friction (opposes motion), tension.
ENERGY / WORKW = F·d. Kinetic KE = ½mv². Work done = change in energy.
FORMULA SHEETp=mv · F·Δt=Δp · f=μN · W=Fd · KE=½mv² · ΣF=ma
