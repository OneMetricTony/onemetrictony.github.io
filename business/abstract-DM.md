## What this program is

A practitioner-led, AI-powered 960-hour diploma built around the reality that **search itself has changed**. Every classical SEO/SEM lesson is paired with its **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** counterpart, grounded in 2024-2026 arxiv research on AI Overviews ranking, autobidding mechanism design, and multi-channel attribution. Every paid-media unit covers Google Ads Performance Max + AI Max + Demand Gen and the auction theory beneath them. Every analytics unit teaches GA4 + GTM (web + server-side) + BigQuery + natural-language-to-SQL. The program opens with a 56-hour AI Foundations bootcamp so non-coders, newcomers, and career-changers leave with durable AI principles before any tool tutorial begins. Hours are mapped chapter-by-chapter against Chaffey/Ellis-Chadwick 9e (Pearson), Kingsnorth 4e (Kogan Page), Hanlon 3e (SAGE), Kaushik *Web Analytics 2.0*, Cutroni *Google Analytics Breakthrough*, and Tuckwell/Crane *Canadian Marketing in Action*.

McKinsey identifies ~70% of AI's enterprise value pool in core marketing functions. BCG documents 3-6× ROI from generative-AI operating-model reinvention.
