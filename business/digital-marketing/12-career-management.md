# 12 — Career Management

**Hours / weeks:** 80 hours / 4 weeks (final term, capstone-adjacent). Shared with the BAM diploma.

## Learning Outcomes

On completion, students will:
1. Conduct a structured self-assessment and map results to Ontario NOC codes and target sectors via Ontario Job Bank.
2. Produce ATS-aware, role-tailored resumes, cover letters, and a LinkedIn profile that pass keyword/parsing checks.
3. Execute a multi-channel Ontario job search including newcomer pathways (credential recognition via WES/ICAS, bridging programs).
4. Demonstrate interview, salary-negotiation, and onboarding competence including disclosure obligations under Ontario's Bill 149 (Working for Workers Four Act).
5. Build a 12-month professional development plan with measurable goals.
6. Maintain PARIS-ready employment evidence supporting Dalwin's PCCA 2005 KPI reporting.

## Weekly Topic Breakdown

- **W1:** Self-assessment + labour-market scan; career portfolio scaffolding; newcomer credential recognition.
- **W2:** Resume/cover letter/LinkedIn build; ATS literacy; personal branding (digital-marketing focus).
- **W3:** Active job search; networking + informational interviews; mock interviews.
- **W4:** Negotiation; onboarding 30/60/90 plan; ongoing PD; PARIS exit documentation.

## AI Integration (tool-agnostic, categories with examples)

- *Resume optimization vs ATS:* keyword-gap analyzers (e.g., Jobscan-style match scoring).
- *Mock-interview agents:* conversational AI for behavioural/technical drills.
- *AI-tailored cover letters:* prompt-engineered drafts edited and fact-checked.
- *Networking outreach:* AI-assisted personalized InMail/email drafting.
- *LinkedIn content:* AI-suggested headlines, About sections, weekly thought-leadership posts.

## Hands-on Projects

1. **Job-Ready Portfolio** (DM: campaign book, analytics report, content samples) — peer-juried.
2. **Mock Interview Reel** — three recorded sessions with self-critique and AI-feedback log.
3. **90-Day Onboarding & 12-Month PD Plan** tied to a real target offer or simulated role.

## Assessment & AI-Use Policy

Portfolio 30% · mock-interview reel 20% · applied job-search log (10 documented applications + outreach) 25% · negotiation/onboarding plan 15% · professionalism 10%.
Every deliverable requires an **AI-Use Disclosure Statement** modelling Bill 149 disclosure norms.

## Pre/co-requisites

Completion of Term 1-2 courses; co-requisite with capstone/practicum.

## Instructor Depth Required

Minimum 5 years Ontario hiring/recruiting or career-coaching experience; current familiarity with ATS platforms, Ontario employment law (ESA, Bill 149), newcomer settlement pathways.
