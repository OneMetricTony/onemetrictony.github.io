# 06 — Project Management

**Hours / weeks:** 80 hours / 4 weeks (intensive block). Shared core with the Business Administration & Management diploma.

## Learning Outcomes

By completion, learners will be able to:
1. Apply the twelve PMBOK 7th edition principles and eight performance domains to plan and govern a small-to-mid project end-to-end.
2. Produce core artifacts: project charter, WBS, schedule, budget estimate, RAID log, communications plan, and closure report.
3. Compare and select predictive, Agile/Scrum, Kanban, and hybrid delivery approaches against project context and stakeholder needs.
4. Facilitate Scrum events (sprint planning, daily standup, review, retrospective) and run a Kanban board with WIP limits.
5. Integrate AI assistants ethically into planning, risk, status, and meeting workflows while preserving human accountability.
6. Communicate project status, escalations, and value delivery to technical and non-technical stakeholders.

## Weekly Topic Breakdown

- **W1:** Project lifecycles & PMBOK 7 principles; initiation, business case, stakeholder identification, charter drafting.
- **W2:** Scope & WBS; estimation; scheduling; cost baselining; risk; stakeholder engagement & communications plan.
- **W3:** Adaptive delivery: Agile Manifesto, Scrum roles/events/artifacts, Kanban flow, hybrid tailoring; team dynamics and servant leadership.
- **W4:** Monitoring/control, EVM basics, status reporting & dashboards, change control, closure, lessons learned; capstone presentations.

## AI Integration (tool-agnostic; examples illustrative)

- AI-assisted planning/estimation: generate draft WBS and three-point estimates from a charter.
- Status report generation: synthesize task/board/timesheet exports into a stakeholder-ready weekly status.
- Risk surface scanning: prompt an LLM to enumerate category-based risks.
- Meeting intelligence/summaries: AI transcription producing action items and decisions.
- RAID log automation: AI extracts Risks/Assumptions/Issues/Dependencies from meeting notes and emails.

## Hands-on Projects

1. End-to-end charter + plan package for a simulated Markham client (DM emphasis: website relaunch).
2. Two-sprint Scrum simulation in cross-functional teams.
3. Status & closure deliverable: AI-drafted weekly status reports + final closure report.

## Assessment & AI-Use Policy

Charter/plan 25% · Scrum simulation 20% · status report portfolio 15% · risk + estimation quiz 10% · capstone presentation 20% · professionalism/participation 10%.
AI permitted with disclosure block. Prohibited for closed-book quizzes.
