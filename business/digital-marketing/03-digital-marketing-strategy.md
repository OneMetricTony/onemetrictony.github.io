# 03 — Digital Marketing Strategy

**Hours / weeks:** 120 hours / 6 weeks (core capstone-feeder course; ~20 hrs/week).

## Learning Outcomes

By course end, students will be able to:
1. Construct an integrated digital marketing strategy aligned to SMART objectives, buyer personas, and customer journey stages using frameworks such as SOSTAC, RACE, and Jobs-to-be-Done.
2. Design and defend a channel mix across SEO, SEM, paid social, email, affiliate, and influencer — with budget allocation rationale tied to funnel stage and CAC/LTV math.
3. Build SEO and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) plans that earn visibility in both classic SERPs and AI Overviews / LLM citation surfaces (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini).
4. Architect Google Ads campaigns using the current AI-driven stack (Performance Max, AI Max for Search, Demand Gen) and Meta Advantage+, including asset groups, audience signals, and bidding strategies.
5. Implement a measurement and attribution plan covering GA4, Consent Mode v2, server-side tagging, and Canadian privacy/anti-spam obligations (PIPEDA, CASL, Quebec Law 25).
6. Translate analytics into iteration: reporting dashboards, experiment backlogs, and budget reallocation decisions defensible to a client or executive.

## Weekly Topic Breakdown

- **Wk 1 — Strategy & Audience:** SOSTAC/RACE, situation analysis, ICP and persona research, JTBD interviews, market sizing, competitive teardown, brand positioning, OKRs/KPIs.
- **Wk 2 — Channel Strategy & Owned Media:** Channel selection logic, full-funnel mapping, SEO fundamentals in the AI Overviews era (E-E-A-T, topical authority, 200-300 word answer chunks, schema, llms.txt, AI crawler policy), email lifecycle, affiliate and influencer models.
- **Wk 3 — Paid Media I (Search & Performance):** Google Ads account architecture, keyword strategy, AI Max for Search, Performance Max asset groups and audience signals, Smart Bidding (tCPA, tROAS, Max Conversions), negative keyword and brand exclusion governance, budget pacing.
- **Wk 4 — Paid Media II (Demand & Social):** Demand Gen (YouTube, Discover, Gmail, Shoppable CTV), Meta Advantage+ Shopping and App, creative testing matrices, the "Power Pack" budget split (PMax 50-60 / AI Max 30-40 / Demand Gen 10-20 for ecom).
- **Wk 5 — Content & Conversion Architecture:** Pillar/cluster content, GEO content briefs, landing page CRO, offer design, lead magnets, conversion rate experimentation, server-side tagging, enhanced conversions for leads.
- **Wk 6 — Measurement, Reporting & Iteration:** GA4 events and conversions, Consent Mode v2 (ad_storage, analytics_storage, ad_user_data, ad_personalization), modelled conversions, data-driven attribution vs MMM, CASL express/implied consent and unsubscribe mechanics, Quebec Law 25, executive dashboards, iteration cadence.

## AI Integration (categories with examples)

- *Keyword & topic research:* clustering tools that surface SERP intent and entity gaps.
- *Content briefs & drafting:* LLM-generated outlines reviewed against E-E-A-T checklists; human-written narrative and citations required.
- *Generative Engine Optimization (GEO):* structuring answer-shaped passages, schema, and citation hooks so content is retrieved by AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity.
- *Ad copy & creative variants:* batch generation of RSA headlines, PMax assets, and Demand Gen video scripts; human QA for claims and brand voice.
- *Bidding & budget:* Smart Bidding signals, value rules, and audience signals fed to PMax/Demand Gen.
- *Attribution & MMM:* AI-assisted data-driven attribution interpretation and lightweight MMM scripts.
- *Competitor intel:* ethical scraping respecting robots.txt, ToS, and PIPEDA — no credential bypass, no PII harvesting.

## Hands-on Projects

1. **Full GTM Digital Strategy** — 12-month plan with personas, channel mix, budget, KPIs, risk register for a Canadian SMB.
2. **Google Ads Build** — Live PMax + AI Max for Search campaign with asset groups, audience signals, conversion goals, and a creative test matrix (sandbox/MCC).
3. **SEO + GEO Content Plan** — 20-piece pillar/cluster plan with AI Overviews optimization, schema spec, and llms.txt proposal.

## Assessment & AI-Use Policy

Projects 50% · weekly deliverables 25% · final strategy defense 15% · participation 10%.
AI tools permitted and encouraged; students must disclose tools used, attach prompts/transcripts, and personally verify every factual claim and data point. Undisclosed AI use or fabricated citations is academic misconduct under Dalwin's policy. All work must respect platform ToS, PIPEDA/CASL/Law 25, and copyright.

## Pre/co-requisites

Marketing Fundamentals; Digital Marketing Foundations. Co-req: Analytics & Data Literacy.

## Instructor Depth Required

Minimum 5 years hands-on paid search/SEO practitioner experience; current Google Ads Search + Measurement certifications; demonstrated PMax/Demand Gen build history; working knowledge of GTM, server-side tagging, Consent Mode v2, and Canadian CASL/PIPEDA compliance.
