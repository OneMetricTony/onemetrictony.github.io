# 09 — Customer Experience

**Hours / weeks:** 80h / 4 weeks.

## Learning Outcomes

1. Apply foundational CX frameworks (Heart of the Customer, Forrester CX Index, Service-Dominant Logic) to diagnose experience gaps across omnichannel touchpoints.
2. Produce journey maps, personas, and service blueprints grounded in qualitative and quantitative Voice-of-Customer (VoC) evidence.
3. Design and prototype AI-assisted support workflows (chatbot, agent-assist, deflection) that respect human-in-the-loop escalation.
4. Select and calculate appropriate CX metrics (CSAT, NPS, CES, retention, first-contact resolution) and tie them to revenue outcomes.
5. Operate within Ontario PCCA 2005, PIPEDA, CASL, and emerging Canadian AI transparency norms when deploying customer-facing AI.
6. Build a closed-loop improvement system (capture → analyze → act → measure).

## Weekly Topic Breakdown

- **Wk 1 (20h):** CX fundamentals; brand-experience economics; persona development; qualitative/quantitative VoC research methods (interviews, surveys, review mining).
- **Wk 2 (20h):** Journey mapping (current/future state); service blueprinting; moments-of-truth; channel orchestration; shift from static maps to journey intelligence.
- **Wk 3 (20h):** Support automation; AI chatbots and agentic systems; intent design; agent-assist; escalation paths; knowledge-base hygiene.
- **Wk 4 (20h):** Measurement (CSAT/NPS/CES/FCR/CLV); AI quality assurance; predictive churn; closed-loop feedback; governance and compliance.

## AI Integration (categories with examples, tool-agnostic)

- **Conversational chatbots/agents:** rule-based FAQ bots, retrieval-augmented LLM assistants, multi-agent orchestration for complex resolutions.
- **VoC mining:** LLM-based thematic analysis of tickets, reviews, transcripts; clustering of verbatims.
- **Sentiment & emotion analytics:** real-time call/chat scoring; emotion-aware routing.
- **AI quality assurance:** automated 100%-coverage scoring of agent interactions against rubrics.
- **Predictive analytics:** churn propensity, next-best-action, escalation likelihood.
- **Agent-assist copilots:** real-time response suggestions and knowledge surfacing.

## Hands-on Projects

1. **SMB Journey Map (Wk 2):** Partner with a Markham-area small business; deliver current-state map, pain points, and prioritized future-state blueprint.
2. **AI Chatbot Design Spec (Wk 3):** Intent taxonomy, conversation flows, fallback/escalation logic, disclosure copy, evaluation plan; no specific platform required.
3. **VoC Mining Report (Wk 4):** Analyze a public review corpus using an AI summarizer; deliver themes, sentiment trends, and three improvement recommendations with KPI targets.

## Assessment & AI-Use Policy

Projects 50% · weekly labs 20% · written midterm 15% · final reflective portfolio 15%.
AI tools encouraged for ideation, drafting, and analysis; students must (a) disclose AI use per artifact, (b) cite prompts and model family, (c) verify all factual claims, (d) never submit unedited AI output as final work.

## Pre/co-requisites

Pre: Digital Marketing Foundations, Consumer Behaviour. Co: Marketing Analytics.

## Instructor Depth Required

5+ years CX, contact-center, or service-design practice; familiarity with at least one CCaaS and one conversational-AI platform; working knowledge of PIPEDA, CASL; CCXP credential or equivalent preferred.
