# 11 — Digital Marketing & Social Selling

**Hours / weeks:** 80 hours / 4 weeks (20 h/wk: lecture, lab, project studio).

## Learning Outcomes

1. Design and execute integrated organic + paid campaigns across LinkedIn, X, Instagram, TikTok, Meta, and YouTube aligned to a documented buyer journey and measurable KPIs.
2. Build a social-selling motion (ICP, signal-based prospecting, multi-touch cadence, conversation handoff) that complies with each platform's ToS and Canadian PIPEDA/CASL rules.
3. Apply modern SEO and SEM fundamentals (technical, on-page, topical authority, AI-overview optimization, keyword-less Search) to plan content and Google Ads campaigns.
4. Use AI tooling for research, creative variant generation, copy refinement, and conversation intelligence while maintaining brand voice, factual accuracy, and ethical scraping practices.
5. Interpret cross-channel attribution (last-click, data-driven, MMM-lite, incrementality tests) and present optimization decisions to a non-technical stakeholder.
6. Produce a written social-selling playbook and a paid-media test plan suitable for a first marketing or BDR role.

## Weekly Topic Breakdown

- **Week 1 — Foundations & Platform Landscape:** Buyer journey, ICP and JTBD, content pillars; platform-by-platform mechanics and 2026 algorithm posture for LinkedIn (authority + AI-assisted Sales Navigator workflows), X, Instagram, TikTok, Meta Feeds/Reels, YouTube long-form and Shorts; SEO fundamentals incl. AI-overview / answer-engine optimization; content frameworks (hook-context-payoff, PAS, problem-agitate-solve, carousel and short-form scripting).
- **Week 2 — Social Selling & Outbound:** Social selling vs. cold outbound; signal-based prospecting (job change, funding, hiring, intent); building cadences across InMail/email/voice notes; social CRM hygiene and pipeline stages; CASL consent, PIPEDA handling of personal data, and platform ToS limits on scraping/automation; objection handling and conversation handoff to sales; metrics (reply rate, meeting rate, SQL conversion, SSI-style authority signals).
- **Week 3 — Paid Social & Search Fundamentals:** Account structure, audiences, creative testing matrices; Meta Advantage+ consolidated campaigns and creative-led optimization; Google Ads Performance Max and keyword-less AI Max Search; YouTube and TikTok video ad mechanics; budget pacing, frequency, creative fatigue; landing-page and offer fit; Consent Mode, server-side tagging, and first-party data strategy.
- **Week 4 — Attribution, AI Stack & Capstone Studio:** Last-click vs. data-driven vs. MMM-lite vs. incrementality lift; UTM governance and dashboarding; AI integration across the stack (see below); A/B and holdout test design; reporting narratives for non-marketers; capstone presentations and instructor critique.

## AI Integration (categories with examples)

- Content generation and repurposing (such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini).
- Audience and account research / ethical enrichment respecting platform ToS and CASL (such as Clay-style data orchestration, Apollo-style B2B databases, Perplexity-style research agents).
- Ad-creative variants and asset multiplication (such as Midjourney, Runway, ElevenLabs, Meta/Google built-in generative ad assets).
- Conversation intelligence and call coaching (such as Gong-style or Chorus-style platforms).
- AI SDR / autonomous outbound agents (such as 11x-, Artisan-, AiSDR-style and Outreach/Salesloft copilots).
- Performance forecasting and creative scoring.

## Hands-on Projects

1. **Multi-platform Campaign:** 2-week organic + paid campaign for a real local SMB or simulated brand, with AI-assisted creative variants, briefs, and a measurement plan.
2. **Social-Selling Playbook:** ICP, signal list, 12-touch cadence, AI-prospecting workflow, ToS/CASL review, and 25 documented outreach attempts with reply analysis.
3. **A/B Test & Attribution Report:** Hypothesis, test design, results, statistical caveats, and a recommendation memo to a stakeholder persona.

## Assessment & AI-Use Policy

Project 1 (25%) · Project 2 (25%) · Project 3 (20%) · labs (15%) · final practical exam (15%).
AI use is required on all deliverables; students must submit a disclosure log naming tools, prompts, and edits, plus a scraping/automation ethics statement citing the relevant platform ToS, PIPEDA, and CASL clauses.

## Pre/co-requisites

Pre-req: Digital Marketing Strategy; Business Communications. Co-req: Content Strategy.

## Instructor Depth Required

Active practitioner with 5+ years running paid social and Google Ads at scale, current hands-on use of an AI SDR or data-orchestration stack, and demonstrable SEO/AI-search portfolio.
