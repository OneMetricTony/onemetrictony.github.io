# 08 — Business Marketing

**Hours / weeks:** 80 hours / 4 weeks. Shared with the Business Administration & Management diploma.

## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion, learners will be able to:
1. Apply core marketing frameworks (STP, 4Ps/7Ps, customer journey) to develop a coherent marketing strategy for a Canadian SMB.
2. Conduct primary and secondary market research, including AI-assisted synthesis of competitor and SERP data, and validate findings against bias and hallucination risk.
3. Translate consumer-behaviour and brand-positioning insights into integrated marketing communications (IMC) plans across paid, owned, and earned channels.
4. Evaluate marketing performance using funnel, attribution, and brand-health metrics, and recommend reallocation of budget.
5. Apply Canadian regulatory requirements — notably CASL express/implied consent, identification, and unsubscribe rules — to campaign design and lead capture.
6. Use generative AI responsibly in market research, persona generation, creative ideation, and segmentation.

## Weekly Topic Breakdown

- **Week 1:** Marketing fundamentals, value creation, environmental scan (PESTLE), market research methods, AI-assisted synthesis.
- **Week 2:** Consumer behaviour, jobs-to-be-done, STP, predictive/AI segmentation.
- **Week 3:** 4Ps/7Ps, pricing psychology, channel and distribution, brand architecture and positioning.
- **Week 4:** Integrated marketing communications, marketing metrics/KPIs, CASL and PIPEDA basics, capstone presentations.

## AI Integration (tool-agnostic; examples illustrative)

- *Market research synthesis:* prompt an LLM to cluster 50 scraped competitor product pages into positioning themes; verify with manual sampling.
- *Persona generation:* synthesize 3 ICPs from interview transcripts and Google Ads search-term reports.
- *Creative ideation:* generate 20 value-prop angles, then human-edit to 3 testable variants.
- *Copy variants:* produce headline/CTA permutations for A/B testing.
- *Predictive segmentation:* score a lead list by likelihood-to-convert based on firmographic and SEO/keyword-intent signals.

## Hands-on Projects

1. **AI-Assisted Go-to-Market Plan (40%)** for a fictitious Markham SMB: market sizing, STP, 4Ps, IMC plan, CASL-compliant lead-capture flow, and a one-page metrics dashboard mock-up.
2. **Brand Positioning Deck (25%)** including competitor SERP/scraping analysis, positioning statement, messaging house, and 3 AI-generated creative concepts with human revisions tracked.

## Assessment & AI-Use Policy

Projects 65% · quizzes 15% · participation/in-class labs 20%.
AI use is permitted and encouraged; learners must disclose tools, prompts, and the human edits made.

## Pre/co-requisites

Business Communications (co-req acceptable); Computer Applications.

## Instructor Depth Required

Minimum 5 years industry marketing experience (agency or in-house), demonstrated familiarity with generative AI workflows, working knowledge of CASL/PIPEDA.
