# 04 — Human Resources

**Hours / weeks:** 80 hours over 4 weeks (20 h/week: lecture 8h, lab 8h, applied project 4h).

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of the course, students will be able to:
1. Apply the Ontario *Employment Standards Act, 2000* (ESA), *Human Rights Code*, *AODA*, *OHSA*, and *PIPEDA* across the employee lifecycle, including the 2026 ESA job-posting amendments (Bill 149 / Working for Workers Four).
2. Design end-to-end talent acquisition workflows (job analysis, sourcing, screening, structured interviewing, offer) that disclose AI use, mitigate algorithmic bias, and meet accessibility duties.
3. Build compensation, performance management, and L&D programs aligned to organizational strategy and Ontario pay-transparency rules.
4. Conduct employee relations, progressive discipline, and termination procedures consistent with ESA minimums and common-law reasonable notice.
5. Interpret HR analytics (turnover, time-to-hire, DEI representation, engagement) and present recommendations to non-HR stakeholders.
6. Evaluate AI-enabled HR tools against an EEDI, privacy, and human-oversight checklist.

## Weekly Topic Breakdown

- **Week 1 — Foundations & Talent Acquisition:** HR strategy; ESA + Human Rights Code primer; workforce planning; job analysis; sourcing channels; AI-assisted resume screening; Ontario AI-disclosure statement on publicly advertised postings (25+ employee threshold, Jan 1, 2026).
- **Week 2 — Onboarding, Compensation & Performance:** AODA-compliant onboarding; total rewards; pay transparency (salary ranges on postings); goal setting; structured reviews; AI-summarized 1:1 notes; calibration sessions.
- **Week 3 — Learning & Development + Employee Relations:** Competency models; personalized L&D pathways; coaching; workplace investigations; progressive discipline; OHSA workplace violence/harassment; accommodation duty; unionized vs. non-union environments.
- **Week 4 — Compliance, Analytics & Offboarding:** Terminations and severance under ESA; record-keeping; people analytics dashboards; predictive attrition; AI governance, vendor due diligence, and the 45-day candidate-notification rule; capstone presentations.

## AI Integration (tool-agnostic, examples only)

- *Resume screening / sourcing:* keyword and skills-match models (e.g., concepts behind HireVue, Workday Talent, Eightfold) — covered with mandatory ESA disclosure language.
- *Interview intelligence:* transcription + structured-scoring assistants (e.g., Metaview-style tools) with human-final-decision rule.
- *Sentiment / engagement:* pulse-survey NLP (e.g., Culture Amp-style) — PIPEDA consent, no individual surveillance.
- *L&D personalization:* adaptive learning paths (e.g., Docebo, LinkedIn Learning-style recommendations).
- *HR analytics:* attrition prediction, DEI representation gap analysis.

Explicit unit on Ontario's 2026 ESA AI-disclosure rule, OECD-aligned AI definition, Human Rights Code adverse-effect discrimination risk, and vendor bias-audit questions.

## Hands-on Projects

1. **Compliant AI-Augmented Hiring Playbook** — draft a job posting (with AI-use and salary-range disclosures), screening rubric, structured interview guide, and bias-mitigation checklist for a fictional Markham SME.
2. **People Analytics Dashboard Mock** — using a synthetic dataset, build a turnover/DEI/time-to-hire dashboard and a one-page executive brief.
3. **HR Compliance Audit Memo** — audit a scenario employer against ESA, Human Rights Code, AODA, and Bill 149; recommend remediation.

## Assessment & AI-Use Policy

Quizzes 15% · Hiring Playbook 25% · Analytics Dashboard 20% · Compliance Memo 20% · Final exam 20%.
Generative AI permitted for drafting and analysis with disclosure of prompts and tools used; AI-generated content must be human-verified, cited, and edited — undisclosed AI use is academic misconduct.

## Pre/co-requisites

Introduction to Business and Business Communications (pre); Organizational Behaviour (co).

## Instructor Depth Required

CHRP/CHRL designation or equivalent, 5+ years Ontario HR generalist experience, working knowledge of HRIS/ATS platforms and HR analytics; PCCA 2005 instructor approval on file.
