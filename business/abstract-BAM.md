## What this program is

A practitioner-led, AI-integrated 960-hour diploma for Ontario adult learners — newcomers, career-changers, and recent grads. It opens with a **56-hour AI Foundations bootcamp** (principles-only, tool-agnostic, no vendor lock) so graduates remain employable as tools change every six months. Each of the twelve specialized courses follows a **[MAIN IDEA] → [MAIN IDEA + AI]** structure: students first learn the business function the way a current Canadian textbook teaches it, then learn how AI is reshaping that function in 2026, then build with AI under explicit Canadian regulatory guardrails. Hours are mapped chapter-by-chapter against Ebert/Griffin (Pearson), Weygandt/Kimmel (Wiley), Belcourt/Schwind (Cengage/McGraw-Hill), Kotler/Tuckwell (Pearson), PMBOK 7 (PMI), and Langton/Robbins (Pearson).

## Why it exists now

WEF *Future of Jobs 2025* ranks AI/Big Data as the #1 fastest-growing skill. BCG documents that 5h of AI training pushes regular use from 67% → 79%. McKinsey reports 46% of organizations cite skill gaps as the #1 blocker to AI scaling. Ontario Bill 149 (effective Jan 1 2026) mandates AI disclosure on hiring postings. Most Ontario PCC competitors teach **zero** AI; public colleges teach implicit AI inside legacy modules. This program closes the gap with explicit AI titles, principles-first pedagogy, and Canadian-compliance literacy from day one.
